I build tools for coordinating humans and AI agents.

My focus is designing workflows, processes, and protocols that allow teams of humans and agents to collaborate transparently and efficiently.

## Current project: AYNIG

**AYNIG (Agentic Yet Native in Git)** turns Git into a coordination engine for teams of humans and AI agents.

🌐 https://aynig.org  
📦 https://github.com/hacknlove/all-you-need-is-git

Commits carry state.  
Humans and agents react to it.

Instead of locking teams into monolithic platforms, AYNIG follows a Unix-style philosophy: simple primitives, composable tools, and full ownership of the workflow.
